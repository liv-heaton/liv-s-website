About Me

Who am I?

My name is Liv Heaton, I'm about to graduate high school in Sydney's north-west. I have an avid interest in computers, both software and hardware. It all began when I was eleven and broke my foot and couldn't walk for a few months. My dad bought me a book to learn Python from, and by the time I could walk again, I had a moderate understanding of Python.

As an adult, I am still obsessed with coding. I blame my father. I have created multiple video games as projects, both 3D and 2D. I was the webmaster for my school's VET showcase and gained a deep understanding of HTML and CSS.
